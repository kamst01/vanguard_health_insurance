What is Term Insurance?

Term health insurance is purchased for a defined period of time and generally has a much lower monthly premium than other forms of major medical health insurance. Term Health Insurance typically covers many benefits including Doctor/Specialist visits, Lab tests, X-Rays, hospitalization, and emergency care.
