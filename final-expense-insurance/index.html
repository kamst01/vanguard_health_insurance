Final Expense Insurance

What is Final Expense Insurance?

Final Expense Insurance is a whole life insurance that has a death benefit and is easier to get approved for. Final expense insurance is also called "funeral insurance", "burial insurance", "simplified issue whole life insurance" or "modified whole life insurance." They are typically small whole life policies with a face value (death benefit) of $2,000 to $50,000.

Benefits of Final Expense Insurance

1.

Does not require a health exam.

2.

Application typically asks few health-related questions.

3.

You can't be turned down for certain policy types, such as "guaranteed issue life insurance".

4.

Premiums will not increase over time.
